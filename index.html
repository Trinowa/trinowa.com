Training Programs

Modular Mainframe Systems Programmer Training

Delivered as bootcamps, cohorts, or continuous learning tracks — we build confident, console‑ready mainframe professionals.

Track 01

Mainframe Foundations

Graduates, lateral hires & IT professionals new to IBM Z

  • Enterprise computing & IBM Z introduction
  • z/OS overview, ISPF navigation, datasets & utilities
  • Basic JCL structure, job submission & monitoring
  • Intro to JES2, DB2, CICS & MQ concepts

Outcomes

Navigate z/OS, run basic jobs, and read job output confidently.

Track 02

Operations & Scheduling

Operators, production control, NOC & L1/L2 support

  • Console operations, message handling & incident response
  • Job scheduling, dependencies & calendar management
  • JES2 commands, SDSF monitoring & output management
  • Change, release & runbook execution best practices

Outcomes

Manage daily operations, job flows, alerts, and follow standard procedures.

Track 03

z/OS Basics – Systems Programmers

Junior / aspiring z/OS systems programmers & admins

  • z/OS architecture, address spaces, storage & workload management
  • System libraries, PARMLIB, PROCLIB & SMP/E concepts
  • IPL, shutdown, HMC basics & JES2/JES3 subsystem overview
  • Intro to WLM, RMF, basic performance & availability

Outcomes

Assist with IPLs, parameter changes, software maintenance, and performance checks.

Track 04

IAM (RACF) – Security Administration

Security admins, z/OS sysptogs & auditors

  • Security concepts, SAF, RACF database & classes
  • User & group administration, attributes & access levels
  • Dataset & general resource protection, profiles & naming
  • SETROPTS, auditing, SMF reporting & zSecure tooling

Outcomes

Manage RACF users/groups, secure datasets & CICS resources, and audit security posture.

Track 05

CICS / MQ / DB2 – Systems Programmers

z/OS sysprogs & systems DBAs

  • CICS region structure, PLT, resource definitions & task management
  • CICS security, monitoring, problem determination & recovery
  • MQ architecture, queue managers, channels & triggering
  • Db2 subsystem architecture, logging, buffer pools & tuning
  • Installation, migration, backup/recovery & DR strategies

Outcomes

Install, configure, maintain CICS, MQ & Db2 subsystems and troubleshoot production issues.

Track 06

Networking (z/OS Comms Server)

Sysprogs & network specialists on IBM Z

  • Networking fundamentals on IBM Z, TCP/IP architecture & protocols
  • z/OS Communications Server TCP/IP: profiles, stacks & parameters
  • VTAM/SNA concepts & relationships with CICS and Db2
  • Operator commands, monitoring & network problem determination

Outcomes

Configure z/OS TCP/IP & VTAM networking, monitor connectivity, and troubleshoot issues.

Track 07

Disaster Recovery

Sysprogs, storage admins, Db2 admins & ops leads

  • DR concepts: RTO/RPO, hot/warm/cold sites & end‑to‑end planning
  • z/OS & Db2 recovery strategies, log‑based & conditional restart
  • Volume dumps, DASD mirroring, XRC/Global Mirror & failover
  • DR runbooks, drills & coordinated recovery with CICS, IMS & Db2

Outcomes

Lead mainframe DR planning, execute procedures, and validate recovery through structured tests.

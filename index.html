About Trinowa

Your Partner in Mainframe Excellence

Trinowa is a specialist provider of Mainframe Systems Programming Services and Enterprise Training, delivering deep technical expertise across production environments in banking, transportation, insurance, and other mission-critical industries. With over 18+ years of real-world mainframe infrastructure experience, we support and develop high-performance teams in Systems Programming (z/OS), Production Control & Scheduling, Security Administration (RACF/IAM), Networking (z/OS Communications Server), Middleware (CICS, MQ, Db2), and Disaster Recovery & Resilience.

We combine hands-on operational knowledge with structured, outcome-driven learning programs to create professionals who are confident on the console, in change windows, during audits, and under disaster recovery pressure.

Enterprise Training Programs

Modular Mainframe Systems Programming Training

Each track is designed around real production scenarios, audit expectations, and DR readiness.

Track 01

Mainframe Foundations

Early Talent, Graduates, Lateral Hires New to IBM Z

  • Introduction to enterprise computing & IBM Z
  • z/OS overview, ISPF navigation, datasets & utilities
  • Basic JCL structure, job submission & monitoring
  • Intro to JES2, Db2, CICS, and MQ concepts

Outcomes

Participants confidently Navigate z/OS, Submit and monitor jobs, Interpret job output, Understand core mainframe architecture.

Track 02

Mainframe Operations & Scheduling

Operators, Production Control, NOC, L1/L2 Support

  • Console operations & incident handling
  • Job scheduling & dependency management
  • JES2 commands & SDSF monitoring
  • Change & release execution best practices

Outcomes

Teams can Manage daily production flows, Respond to alerts, Execute runbooks, Maintain SLA stability.

Track 03

z/OS Basics – Systems Programmers

Junior / Aspiring Systems Programmers

  • z/OS architecture & address spaces
  • Storage & workload management
  • PARMLIB, PROCLIB, SMP/E maintenance
  • IPL, shutdown, JES subsystem overview
  • WLM, RMF & availability concepts

Outcomes

Participants can Assist with IPLs, Manage parameter updates, Support maintenance cycles, Conduct basic performance reviews.

Track 04

IAM (RACF) – Security Administration

Security Admins, Systems Programmers, Auditors

  • SAF & RACF architecture
  • User & group administration
  • Dataset & resource protection
  • SETROPTS & auditing
  • SMF reporting & exposure to security tools

Outcomes

Teams can Secure enterprise resources, Manage RACF profiles, Support audits confidently, Strengthen mainframe security posture.

Track 05

CICS / MQ / Db2 – Systems Programming

z/OS Systems Programmers & Systems DBAs

  • CICS region design & resource management
  • MQ architecture & queue management
  • Db2 subsystem structure & performance tuning
  • Installation, migration & DR alignment

Outcomes

Participants can Install & configure subsystems, Manage performance & availability, Troubleshoot production issues, Align middleware with DR strategies.

Track 06

Mainframe Networking (z/OS Communications Server)

Systems Programmers & Network Specialists

  • TCP/IP architecture on IBM Z
  • z/OS Communications Server configuration
  • VTAM/SNA concepts
  • Network problem determination

Outcomes

Teams can Configure TCP/IP stacks, Manage connectivity, Troubleshoot application network issues.

Track 07

Mainframe Disaster Recovery

z/OS Systems Programmers, Storage Teams, Db2 Admins, Operations Leads

  • RTO / RPO planning
  • Hot / Warm / Cold site strategies
  • z/OS & Db2 recovery techniques
  • Storage replication & failover patterns
  • DR drill design & execution

Outcomes

Participants can Lead DR exercises, Execute recovery runbooks, Validate recovery capabilities, Ensure business continuity readiness.
